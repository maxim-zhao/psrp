[Armor Shop]    [▲Top]
 This is an armor shop. Would you like to buy anything?
    Yes    Which [item] would you like?
           <Inventory List>
           Choose item
               <*RING*> 
               Thank you. Anything else?
               Yes    Which [item] would you...
               No     All right. Please come again.
                      <Exit>
           Choose item, not enough money
               It looks like you don't have enough money.
               Please come back later.
               <Exit>
           Choose item, inventory full
               It looks like you can't carry any more items.
               Please come back later.
               <Exit>
           Cancel
               All right. Please come again.
               <Exit>
    No/Cancel
        All right. Please come again.
        <Exit>

[Tool Shop]    [▲Top]
 This is a tool shop. What would you like to do?
    Buy     (Same as Armor Shop)
    Sell    Which [item] would you like to sell?
            <Inventory List>
            Choose Item
                Ah, a(n) [item name]. Is [#] meseta alright?
                Yes    Thank you. Anything else?
                       Yes    Which [item] would you...
                       No     Okay. Please come again.
                              <Exit>
                No     Which [item] would you...
                Cancel
                       Okay. Please come again.
                       <Exit>
    Cancel  Okay. Please come again.
            <Exit>


[Hospital]    [▲Top]
 This is a hospital. Would you like to undergo medical treatment?
    Yes    Who will receive treatment?
           <Alisa/Myau/Tairon/Lutz>
           Choose Character
               Examination and treatment will cost [#] meseta?
               Is that alright?
               Yes    Thank you for your patience (hah hah).
                      <Character healed>
                      There you go. Take care now.
                      Is there anyone else who would like to undergo treatment?
                      Yes    Who will undergo...
                      No     I see. I'm sorry that we cannot help you.
                             Take care now.
                             <Exit>
               Yes, not enough money
                      It looks like you don't have enough money.
                      Please come back later.
                      <Exit>
               No     I see. I'm sorry that we cannot help you.
                      Take care now.
                      <Exit>
           Choose character, not injured
               It appears that (character name) is not injured.
               Who will receive...
           Choose character, dead
               (Character name) is dead.
               Who will receive...
    No     I see. I'm sorry that we cannot help you.
           Take care now.
           <Exit>

                      Artemuhamuramu...artemuhamuramu...storatopon! [??]
